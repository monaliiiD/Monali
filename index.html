Hi, I'm Mayura Manawadu

PhD Student at The University of Melbourne

I research on mixed reality using panoramic RGB-D (360° + LiDAR) captures, focusing on enabling reliable interaction and smooth navigation in real-world 360° scenes. This includes depth-based occlusion, contact, and collision between virtual and captured environments, as well as generating intermediate views that allow movement in-between and through panoramic nodes rather than simple teleportation.
